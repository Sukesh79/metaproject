3d video calling

A 3D conversation system can facilitate 3D conversations in an augmented reality environment, allowing conversation participants to appear as if they are face-to-face. The 3D conversation system can accomplish this with a pipeline of data processing stages, which can include calibrate, capture, tag and filter, compress, decompress, reconstruct, render, and display stages,” the patent reads. As you can imagine, this 3D calling tech will allow you to connect with your loved one in a three-dimensional form instead of a 2D one.
